Description

The player takes on the role of Five, a member of a treasure-hunting team that survives by looting relics from other planets. In the game, Five embarks on a mission to the planet SLANM-05 with the goal of finding and retrieving the element that controls the planet's antigravity and bringing it back to the ship.

To achieve this, Five must venture into the planet's dark caves and confront local life forms that will attempt to defend their territory and prevent the mission from succeeding. Upon collecting the element, the player can harness its power to alter gravity during the game.
